Let's build this together

After traveling to Vietnam so many times, I figured it's time to start learning the language. But Vietnamese is really hard to learn. You can't just read something and remember it. You need to speak it and actively practice it, even more so than other languages because of the tones. So I would use Google Translate when I wanted to practice speaking to see how close I could get to my intention. But since Google Translate isn't really built for learning, it became a little frustrating. So I had the idea to build an app that blends translation and learning, and haixoai was born.
