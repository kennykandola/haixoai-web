Support

How can we help?

haixoai is a Vietnamese translation and language learning app. If you need help with the app, your account, or have feedback, we're here for you.

Contact support

Email us and include your device model, iOS version, and a brief description of the issue. Screenshots or screen recordings help us resolve problems faster.

We aim to respond within 2–3 business days.

Common topics

  • App crashes or bugs — tell us what you were doing when the issue occurred.
  • Microphone or speech features — confirm microphone permission is enabled in iOS Settings > haixoai.
  • Translation or pronunciation feedback — share the phrase you tried and what you expected.
  • Account or data deletion — email us to request deletion of your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
  • Early access & updates — join the waitlist on our home page.
