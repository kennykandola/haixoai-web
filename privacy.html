Privacy Policy

haixoai ("we," "us," or "our") operates the haixoai mobile application and website at haixoai.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app or website.

Please read this policy carefully. By using haixoai, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

  • App name: haixoai
  • Developer: haixoai
  • Contact: kkandola37@gmail.com
  • Website: https://haixoai.com

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at the email above.

2. Information we collect

We collect information that you provide directly, information generated when you use the app, and limited technical information needed to operate our services.

Information you provide

  • Email address — if you join our waitlist, subscribe for updates, or contact support.
  • Account information — if you create an account (for example, name or email), we store what you provide.
  • User content — text, phrases, vocabulary entries, and conversation content you enter or save in the app.
  • Support communications — information you send when you contact us for help.

Information collected when you use the app

  • Audio and speech data — when you use listening, translation, or pronunciation features, the app may access your device microphone and process audio to provide those features. Audio may be converted to text and analyzed to generate translations, feedback, or learning suggestions.
  • Usage data — how you interact with features (for example, screens viewed, features used, and session activity) to improve the app and fix issues.
  • Device and app information — device type, operating system version, app version, language settings, and diagnostic logs needed for performance, security, and troubleshooting.

Information collected on our website

  • Email address — if you submit our early-access or newsletter form.
  • Technical data — standard server and browser logs (such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited) collected by our hosting provider.

We do not knowingly collect precise location data, contacts, photos, or health data unless a feature explicitly requests it and this policy is updated to describe that use.

3. How we use your information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide translation, language learning, pronunciation feedback, and related app features
  • Save your phrases, vocabulary, and learning progress
  • Operate, maintain, and improve haixoai
  • Respond to support requests and communicate about updates
  • Send optional product announcements if you have opted in
  • Monitor for abuse, fraud, and security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

4. How we share information

We may share information only as described below:

  • Service providers — trusted vendors that help us operate the app (for example, cloud hosting, analytics, crash reporting, or AI/speech processing providers). They may process data only on our instructions and for the purposes described in this policy.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business transfers — if we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may transfer as part of that transaction, subject to this policy.

We do not share personal information with third parties for their independent marketing purposes.

5. AI and speech processing

haixoai may send audio, transcripts, or text you provide to third-party AI or speech services to generate translations, conversation responses, or learning feedback. Those providers process data to deliver the requested feature. We select providers that apply appropriate security and confidentiality safeguards.

Do not submit sensitive personal information (such as financial, medical, or government ID information) through speech or text features unless you choose to do so.

6. Data retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide the app, fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

You may request deletion of your account or personal data by contacting us at kkandola37@gmail.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe, subject to legal retention requirements.

To exercise these rights, email kkandola37@gmail.com. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling a request.
